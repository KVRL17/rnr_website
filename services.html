APFC PANEL

Automatic Power Factor Control Panels are primarily used for improving the Power Factor. Power Factor is the ratio of active power to apparent power and is a key factor in measuring electrical consumption. With the rising cost of electricity, it is crucial to reduce electrical consumption to minimize expenses.

Automatic Power Factor Control Panels are essential for achieving this goal, especially in industries where electrical installations supply large electrical loads. A dip in Power Factor can lead to operational losses and penalties from the electricity board responsible for supply.

We offer a wide range of standard and customized APFC panels and remain competitive in the industry. These panels effectively and automatically manage quickly changing and scattered loads while maintaining a high Power Factor.

As renowned manufacturers of APFC Panels, we are synonymous with quality and reliability. Our panels are available in different current ratings to cater to various applications.
