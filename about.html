About Us

Sai Solar Energy is classified as Micro enterprise in the financial year 2023-24. It has its unit situated at Hosur, Tamil Nadu.Being an MNRE-authorized vendor means a company or entity has received approval from the ministry to provide renewable energy solutions that meet government standards. This certification helps ensure quality and reliability in the products and services offered by the vendor. Typically, the government offers subsidies and incentives for projects that use MNRE-authorized vendors, ensuring projects align with national energy goals and sustainability targets.

The major activity of Sai Solar Energy is Manufacturing, Sub-classified into Manufacture of electrical equipment and is primarily engaged in the Manufacture of primary cells and primary batteries and rechargeable batteries cells containing manganese oxide mercuric oxide silver oxide or other material.

Solar energy is a renewable and sustainable power source that harnesses sunlight and converts it into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) panels. As sunlight hits the solar panels, it generates direct current (DC) electricity, which is then converted into alternating current (AC) through an inverter for use in homes, businesses, or industrial applications. Solar energy is clean, reduces greenhouse gas emissions, and helps lower reliance on fossil fuels. It is a versatile energy solution, scalable from small residential systems to large solar farms, and can be paired with battery storage for continuous power, even during cloudy periods or at night.
